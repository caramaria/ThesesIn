Vision

Simple Search

Use the simple search function to find the lectuerer for your thesis

Improve Collaboration

Use the Chat function to get in contact with your academic supervisor

Shared Interests

ThesIn helps you to find the lecturer who is interested in your topic so he is able to support you as best as possible

Central Platform

ThesIn enables you to have all neseccary documents at one central place like your: Documents, Chats, Search & Feeback

Success

With having the best available support we want to help you to write your thesis as best as possible that wou'll be successfull in the future

Mission

To make the process to find the perfect lecturer for yout thesis we want to build a central platform which is accessable for every Prof and every Student within your institute.

About us


We're a group of students at the DHBW Mannheim who felt, that there has to be a simpler and more efficient way to find a lecturer for academic papers.
Therfore we decided to build a plaform which is accesable for students and profs so that they can start to colloberate.
With building this platform we want to make the knowlege, which is available at the DHBW tranparent for both students and profs.
